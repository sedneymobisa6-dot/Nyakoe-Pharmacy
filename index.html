About Nyakoe Chemist

Nyakoe Chemist is Kisii County's most trusted community pharmacy. We have been serving the people of Kisii and the surrounding region with genuine, high-quality medicines and health products.

Our team of qualified pharmacists and health professionals is committed to improving the health and wellbeing of every customer who walks through our doors — or contacts us online.

We stock a wide range of prescription medicines, OTC products, vitamins, beauty, and baby care products at competitive prices.
